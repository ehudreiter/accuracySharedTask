The Indiana Pacers ( 5 - 4 ) defeated the Boston Celtics ( 3 - 4 ) 102 - 91 on Wednesday at the TD Garden in Boston . The Pacers got off to a strong start in this game , out - scoring the Celtics 64 - 48 in the first half alone . They were also able to get it done in the second half , where they out - scored the Celtics 59 - 42 , allowing them to coast to a victory on the road . The Pacers were led by Paul George , who finished with a game - high 26 points ( 10 - 22 FG , 2 - 5 3Pt , 4 - 5 FT ) , along with 10 rebounds , three assists and one steal . CJ Miles chipped in with 17 points ( 6 - 10 FG , 1 - 4 3Pt , 4 - 4 FT ) and five rebounds , while Isaiah Thomas chipped in with 14 points ( 4 - 13 FG , 1 - 4 3Pt , 5 - 7 FT ) , three rebounds and four assists . The Pacers had six players reach double figures in points . Evan Turner led the way , scoring 20 points ( 9 - 13 FG , 0 - 0 3Pt , 2 - 2 FT ) to go along with three rebounds . He 's now combined for 52 points in his last two games , so it was good to see him get back on track . The only other player to reach double figures in points was Isaiah Thomas , who recorded 14 points ( 4 - 13 FG , 1 - 4 3Pt , 5 - 7 FT ) and three rebounds . Boston will look to continue their winning ways on the road Thursday against the Boston Celtics . The Celtics ' next game will be on the road against the Cleveland Cavaliers on Friday , while the Celtics will travel to Indiana to play the Pacers on Wednesday .
