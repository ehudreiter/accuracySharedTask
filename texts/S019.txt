The Miami Heat defeated the Indiana Pacers , 113 - 95 , at American Airlines Arena on Saturday evening . The Heat were expected to win this game easily and they took care of business in this wire - to - wire win . In fact , the Heat led for the entirety of the second half , as they outscored the Pacers , 30 - 16 , in the third quarter . The assist - to - turnover ratio was the decisive statistic , with Miami recording 23 assists to 16 turnovers , while Indiana committed 15 turnovers to just 16 assists . The Pacers ( 30 - 29 ) have now won five of their last six games , as they continue to falter . Paul George led the way for Indiana , as he tallied 10 points , five rebounds and one assist . Jeff Teague was second on the team , finishing with 16 points , three rebounds and six assists . Thaddeus Young was the only other starter in double figures , as he totaled 13 points , five rebounds and four assists . On deck for Indiana is a home matchup against the Atlanta Hawks on Friday . The Heat ( 27 - 32 ) have now won four of their last six games , as they continue to falter . Goran Dragic was the player of the game , as he accumulated 21 points , two rebounds and four assists on 8 - of - 12 shooting . Hassan Whiteside recorded a double - double , totaling 22 points and 17 rebounds . Dion Waiters was the only other starter in double figures , accruing 22 points , two rebounds and three assists . Miami will look to play better on Thursday in a home matchup against the Toronto Raptors .
