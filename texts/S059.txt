The Golden State Warriors defeated the Utah Jazz , 104 - 74 , at Oracle Arena on Saturday . Golden State was expected to win this game easily , but it took a monster second half to escape with a win . In fact , the Warriors led for the entirety of the second half , as they held a double - digit lead for the final 30 - plus minutes . Defense was key for Golden State , as they held the Jazz to 36 percent from the field and 30 percent from three - point range . Rebounding was a huge factor for the Warriors , as they won that battle , 50 - 44 . Kevin Durant was the player of the game , as he tallied 22 points and five rebounds on 8 - of - 14 shooting . Draymond Green finished second on the team with 15 points , 11 rebounds and four assists . Rudy Gobert recorded a double - double , totaling 11 points and 17 rebounds . Klay Thompson led the team in scoring , as he dropped 17 points on 8 - of - 13 shooting . Draymond Green recorded a double - double , totaling 15 points , 11 rebounds , four assists and five steals . Joe Johnson was the only other starter in double figures , as he accrued 14 points and two rebounds . Gordon Hayward was the player of the game , as he accumulated 25 points , three rebounds and four assists . Rudy Gobert was the only other Jazz player in double figures , as he totaled 11 points and 17 rebounds .
