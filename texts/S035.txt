The Oklahoma City Thunder defeated the host Miami Heat , 106 - 94 , at American Airlines Arena on Tuesday . In a battle between two of the best offenses in the NBA , the Thunder were able to prevail with a huge road win . In fact , Oklahoma City outscored Miami , 46 - 34 in the second half to pull out a win . Rebounding was the biggest factor for Oklahoma City , as they won that battle , 46 - 34 . The Thunder shot 51 percent from the field , while holding the Heat to 44 percent from the field . Rebounding was also decisive , with Oklahoma City winning that battle , 46 - 32 . Oklahoma City ( 20 - 12 ) had to play this game without Kevin Durant and they did n't disappoint . Russell Westbrook carried the load , as he accumulated 29 points , 17 rebounds and 11 assists . Steven Adams was second on the team , totaling 15 points , eight rebounds and two blocks . Andre Roberson and Domantas Sabonis were the only other Thunder players in double figures , as they scored just 1 points a piece . Domantas Sabonis played well off the bench , providing six points and five rebounds . On deck for the Thunder is a road matchup with the Orlando Magic on Monday . Miami ( 10 - 22 ) have been one of the best teams in the NBA across the last two months , as this marks their 10th win in their last eight games . Josh Richardson led the team in scoring , as he collected 22 points , five rebounds and three assists off the bench . Tyler Johnson was second on the team , as he accrued 15 points , two rebounds and two assists . Hassan Whiteside totaled 12 points , eight rebounds and two steals . Josh Richardson led the bench in scoring , accruing 22 points , five rebounds and three assists . Miami will host the Boston Celtics for their next game on Saturday .
