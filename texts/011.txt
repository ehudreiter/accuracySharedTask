The Hawks got off to a quick start in this one , out - scoring the Wizards 29 - 18 in the first quarter alone . The Hawks were able to out - score the Wizards 28 - 18 in the third quarter , while the Wizards were able to coast to a victory in front of their home crowd . It was his second double - double in a row , as he ' s combined for 54 points and 14 rebounds over his last two games .
