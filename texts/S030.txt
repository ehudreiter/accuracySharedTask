The Indiana Pacers defeated the Charlotte Hornets , 110 - 94 , at Bankers Life Fieldhouse on Wednesday . Indiana was expected to win this game easily and they left no doubt with this overtime win . In fact , the Pacers led for the entirety of the second half , as they outscored the Hornets , 34 - 21 , in the fourth quarter . Rebounding was a huge factor in the win , with the Pacers winning that battle , 42 - 34 . The Hornets ( 14 - 11 ) have now won five of their last seven games , as they continue to play well . Kemba Walker did all he could for Charlotte , as he led the team with 12 points , two rebounds and five assists . Nicolas Batum was second on the team , as he accrued 13 points , 10 rebounds and three assists . Cody Zeller was the only other starter in double figures , as he accrued 11 points , seven rebounds and two blocks . On deck for Charlotte is a road matchup with the Los Angeles Clippers on Monday .
