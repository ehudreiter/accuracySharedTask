The Toronto Raptors defeated the Oklahoma City Thunder , 102 - 123 , at Air Canada Center on Friday . In a battle between two of the best offenses in the NBA , the Raptors ( 39 - 29 ) held a double - digit lead for the entirety of the second half . In fact , the Raptors led for the majority of the game , as the Thunder never led by more than 12 points for the entirety of the second half . The Raptors ( 39 - 29 ) had to play this game without Kevin Durant , but they simply did n't have enough bullets to win this game . Russell Westbrook once again carried the load , as he tallied 24 points , 10 rebounds and 16 assists . Victor Oladipo led the team in scoring , as he accrued 23 points , five rebounds and two assists . Enes Kanter was second on the team , totaling 14 points and seven rebounds . Taj Gibson was the only other player in double figures , dropping 10 points and four rebounds . On deck for the Thunder is a road matchup against the Oklahoma City Thunder on Thursday . The Raptors ( 39 - 29 ) have now won five of their last six games , as they continue to climb up the standings . DeMar DeRozan was the player of the game , as he accumulated 22 points , three rebounds and three assists on 8 - of - 14 shooting . Serge Ibaka was second on the team , as he accrued 10 points and three rebounds . Jonas Valanciunas amassed seven points , five rebounds and two blocks . Cory Joseph was a nice spark off the bench , accruing 11 points , two rebounds and six assists . Toronto will host the Boston Celtics for their next game on Thursday .
