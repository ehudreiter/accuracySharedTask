The Boston Celtics ( 1 - 1 ) defeated the Toronto Raptors ( 2 - 0 ) 103 - 113 on Friday at the TD Garden in Boston . The Celtics had a strong first half where they out - scored the Raptors 50 - 38 in the second half to pull away in the second half . The Celtics were led by Isaiah Thomas , who scored a game - high 25 points ( 7 - 16 FG , 1 - 4 3Pt , 10 - 11 FT ) and dished out seven assists in 29 minutes . DeMarre Carroll had a strong showing as well , finishing with 21 points ( 8 - 16 FG , 4 - 7 3Pt ) and five rebounds in 35 minutes . Jonas Valanciunas put up six points and a team - high 10 rebounds in 23 minutes off the bench . The Celtics ' next game is on the road against the Atlanta Hawks on Friday , while the Raptors will travel to Charlotte to take on the Hornets on Friday .
