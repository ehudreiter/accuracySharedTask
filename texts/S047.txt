The Atlanta Hawks ( 25 - 8 ) defeated the Portland Trail Blazers ( 26 - 8 ) 115 - 107 on Tuesday at the Moda Center in Portland . The Hawks came into this game riding a three - game losing streak , but it was n't enough to get the victory in front of their home crowd . The Hawks shot 51 percent from the field , compared to the Blazers who shot 51 percent on their end . The Hawks were led by Paul Millsap , who finished with a game - high of 27 points ( 11 - 17 FG , 0 - 1 3Pt , 5 - 7 FT ) , to go along with six rebounds . Jeff Teague had 22 points ( 8 - 16 FG , 6 - 6 FT ) and six assists , while Millsap added 27 points ( 11 - 17 FG , 0 - 1 3Pt , 5 - 7 FT ) and six rebounds . Kyle Korver chipped in with 14 points ( 5 - 11 FG , 4 - 9 3Pt ) and eight rebounds as well . The Trail Blazers had six players reach double figures in points . Wesley Matthews shot 5 - for - 14 from the field and 4 - for - 10 from the three - point line to score 19 points , while Nicolas Batum added 16 points ( 7 - 14 FG , 2 - 8 3PT ) and a pair of rebounds off the bench . The Blazers shot just 7 - 24 ( 39 percent ) from three - point land , but it was n't enough to propel the Trail Blazers to the win . The Hawks ' next game will be on the road against the Cleveland Cavaliers on Friday , while the Trail Blazers will travel to Cleveland to play the Cavaliers on Friday .
