The New York Knicks defeated the Milwaukee Bucks , 93 - 104 , at BMO Harris Bradley Center on Friday . The Knicks ( 26 - 39 ) came into this game tied for the worst record in the Eastern Conference , but the Knicks ( 30 - 33 ) have now won five of their last six games . The Knicks ( 30 - 33 ) have now won five of their last six games , as they have now lost five of their last six games .
