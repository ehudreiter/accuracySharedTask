The Atlanta Hawks ( 59 - 19 ) defeated the Brooklyn Nets ( 36 - 42 ) 114 - 111 at the Barclays Center on Friday . The Hawks came into this game winners of four of their last five games , and they 've now won five of their last five games . The Nets were led by Al Horford , who tallied 24 points , seven rebounds , four assists , two steals and one block in 33 minutes . DeMarre Carroll had 19 points ( 7 - 13 FG , 3 - 7 3Pt ) , nine rebounds , five assists and one steal in 37 minutes . Brook Lopez led the Nets in scoring with 26 points ( 8 - 17 FG , 10 - 10 FT ) , along with 10 rebounds , two assists , one steal and three blocks , in 39 minutes . Jarrett Jack chipped in 14 points ( 3 - 10 FG , 8 - 8 FT ) , along with three assists , three rebounds and one steal , in 21 minutes . The Nets had six players score in double figures , while shooting a solid 48 percent from the field . Next , the Nets will play in Indiana on Friday . The Hawks will host the Knicks on Friday .
