The Detroit Pistons ( 2 - 3 ) defeated the New York Knicks ( 2 - 3 ) 98 - 95 on Wednesday at the Palace of Auburn Hills in Detroit . The Knicks got off to a quick start in this one , out - scoring the Knicks 25 - 20 in the first half alone . The Pistons were the superior shooters in this game , going 37 percent from the field and 45 percent from the three - point line , while the Knicks went 43 percent from the floor and 45 percent from beyond the arc . The Knicks were led by Kentavious Caldwell-Pope , who finished with a team - high of 20 points ( 8 - 14 FG , 4 - 7 3Pt , 0 - 2 FT ) , while also adding two rebounds and two blocked shots . It was his second double - double in a row , as he 's combined for 43 points and 25 rebounds over his last two games . Tim Hardaway Jr had a solid game . He went 8 - for - 15 from the field and 4 - for - 7 from the three - point line to score 20 points , while also adding three assists . He 's now averaging 12 points and 2 rebounds on the year . Iman Shumpert had 15 points ( 5 - 13 FG , 4 - 6 3Pt , 1 - 2 FT ) and six assists as well . The Pistons ' next game will be on the road against the Toronto Raptors on Friday , while the Pistons will be at home against the Miami Heat on Friday .
