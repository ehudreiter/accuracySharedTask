The Warriors were led by the duo of Stephen Curry and Klay Thompson . Curry went 9 - for - 22 from the field and 3 - for - 11 from the three - point line to score a game - high of 29 points , while also adding eight assists and three rebounds . It was his second double - double in a row , as he 's combined for 45 points and 21 rebounds over his last two games .
