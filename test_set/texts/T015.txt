The Los Angeles Lakers ( 5 - 13 ) defeated the Detroit Pistons ( 3 - 15 ) 106 - 96 on Wednesday at the Palace of Auburn Hills . The Lakers came into this game riding a three - game losing streak , but were able to pull off the win on the road in Detroit . The Lakers were the superior shooters in this game , going 47 percent from the field , while the Pistons went just 37 percent . The Lakers were led by Nick Young , who finished with 19 points ( 5 - 13 FG , 3 - 6 3Pt , 6 - 7 FT ) and 13 rebounds . Greg Monroe was the only other Laker to reach double figures in points , as he went 2 - for - 7 from the field and 4 - for - 6 from the free throw line to score eight points . He 's now combined for 52 points in the last two games , as he continues to stay very consistent for the Lakers . Brandon Jennings chipped in with 10 points ( 2 - 10 FG , 0 - 1 3Pt , 6 - 6 FT ) and Brandon Jennings had 10 points ( 2 - 10 FG , 0 - 1 3Pt , 6 - 6 FT ) off the bench . The Lakers ' next game will be on the road against the Boston Celtics on Friday , while the Pistons will travel to Brooklyn to play the Nets on Friday .
