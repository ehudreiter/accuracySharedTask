The San Antonio Spurs ( 26 - 16 ) defeated the Utah Jazz ( 14 - 27 ) 89 - 69 on Wednesday at Energy Solutions Arena in Salt Lake City . The Spurs got off to a quick start in this one , out - scoring the Jazz 23 - 15 in the first quarter alone . The Spurs were the superior shooters in this game , going 39 percent from the field and 47 percent from the three - point line , while the Jazz went 33 percent from the floor and just 30 percent from beyond the arc . The Spurs were led by a strong performance from Rudy Gobert , who went 5 - for - 10 from the field and 0 - for - 0 from the three - point line to score a game - high of 13 points , while also adding 18 rebounds and four blocked shots in 29 minutes . The only other player to reach double figures in points was Kawhi Leonard , who finished with 12 points ( 4 - 11 FG , 1 - 2 3Pt , 3 - 4 FT ) , nine rebounds and an assist . It was his second double - double in a row , as he 's combined for 36 points and 10 rebounds over that span . Coming off the bench , Aron Baynes chipped in with 12 points ( 4 - 10 FG , 0 - 1 3Pt , 4 - 5 FT ) and 11 rebounds . The Spurs ' next game will be on the road against the Portland Trail Blazers on Friday , while the Jazz will be at home against the Portland Trail Blazers on Friday .
