The Atlanta Hawks defeated the visiting Cleveland Cavaliers , 130 - 135 , at Philips Arena on Wednesday . Atlanta were expected to win this game easily and they left no doubt with this result . In fact , the Hawks led for the majority of the game , as the Cavaliers never led by more than seven points for the entirety of the second half . The Hawks ( 42 - 18 ) had to play this game without Kyrie Irving , but they simply did n't have enough bullets to win this game . LeBron James was the player of the game , as he tallied 38 points , 13 rebounds and eight assists . Kyrie Irving was n't far behind , as he tallied 43 points , nine assists and four steals on 14 - of - 21 shooting . Richard Jefferson and Channing Frye were the only other Cavalier in double figures , scoring 11 points a piece . Channing Frye led the bench in scoring , dropping 11 points and four rebounds . On deck for the Cavaliers is a road matchup against the Orlando Magic on Friday . The Hawks ( 34 - 27 ) have now won five of their last six games , as they continue to fall down the standings . Paul Millsap was the player of the game , as he collected 27 points and nine rebounds . Dennis Schroder was second on the team , as he accrued 17 points and seven assists . Dwight Howard recorded a double - double , amassing 15 points , 10 rebounds and two steals . Ersan Ilyasova was a nice spark off the bench , providing 10 points and eight rebounds .
